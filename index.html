Yi-Lin Tuan

Research Assistant, National Taiwan University

Research Interests Cover Deep Learning, Natural Language and Speech Processing

Biography

Yi-Lin Tuan graduated from National Taiwan University (NTU) in 2017 with a Bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering.

Tuan is working with Prof. Hung-yi Lee on the research of natural language generation, natural language understanding and knowledge inference, especially via deep learning, reinforcement learning and adversarial learning. He is also working with Prof. Lin-shan Lee on the research of spoken language processin with a focus on lyrics recognition.

For more details, you can check my CV/resume. Also if you have any question, please feel free to contact me via email at pascaltuan@gmail.com
