Yi-Lin Tuan

Research Assistant, National Taiwan University

My Research Interests are Machine Learning, Natural Language and Speech Processing

Biography

Yi-Lin Tuan graduated from National Taiwan University (NTU) in 2017 with a BSE.

Tuan is working with Prof. Hung-yi Lee on the research of natural language generation, natural language understanding and knowledge inference (co-advised by Prof. Yun-Nung Chen), primarily using deep learning, reinforcement learning and adversarial learning. He also worked with Prof. Lin-shan Lee on the research of spoken language processing with a focus on lyrics recognition.

For more details, you can check my CV/resume. If there is any question, please feel free to contact me via email at pascaltuan@gmail.com
